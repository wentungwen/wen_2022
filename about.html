Wen Tung
UX Designer

I worked as a UX/UI designer in Taiwan, Taipei for around 2 years, and I'm currently doing a master's degree in New Media Design at Tilburg University in the Netherlands.

I studied marketing strategy as an undergraduate. However, I discovered that I have a greater passion for creating products rather than promoting them. It pushed me to switch to UX design, which I began learning through online platforms such as Udemy and Coursera.

As I dived deeper into the field of design, I found myself particularly drawn to information design. This includes various aspects such as interaction design for functional products, information architecture for hyper-text systems, or dealing with complex user flow. I truly enjoy crafting seamless user experiences, especially for products that contain the complex information.

I also enjoy working on side projects. To make them happen, I self-learnt development skills. I think it's important to have some engineering knowledge to enhance my projects.
