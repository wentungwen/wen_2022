GogoFriend (Product Design) - The local travel guide for foreigners

It is a point-to-point navigation product using an online tour guide. It was the project of the Transportation Data Hackathon event organized by the Taipei Computer Association.
This platform goes beyond just providing information; it's dedicated to ensuring the safe and reliable arrival of users at their destinations. Regardless of the time or location, users can leverage the strength of the community to travel from point A to point B with confidence and assurance.
